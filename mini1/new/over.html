Diet Chart for Overweight
Time Meal
Breakfast 1 cup of oatmeal, 1/2 cup of mixed berries, 1 tablespoon of honey, 1 slice of whole-grain toast, 1 cup of low-fat milk
Morning Snack 1 apple, 10 almonds
Lunch 4 oz of grilled chicken breast, 1 cup of cooked brown rice, 1 cup of mixed vegetables, 1 slice of whole-grain bread
Afternoon Snack 1 cup of baby carrots, 2 tablespoons of hummus
Dinner 4 oz of grilled salmon, 1 sweet potato, 1 cup of steamed broccoli, 1 slice of whole-grain bread
Evening Snack 1 cup of mixed berries, 1/2 cup of low-fat cottage cheese
