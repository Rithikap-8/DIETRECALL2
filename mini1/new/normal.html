Diet Chart for Normal Weight
Time Meal
Breakfast 1 cup of oatmeal, 1/2 cup of mixed berries, 1 tablespoon of honey, 1 slice of whole-grain bread, 1 cup of low-fat milk
Morning Snack 1 apple, 1 tablespoon of almond butter
Lunch 4 oz of grilled chicken breast, 1 cup of cooked quinoa, 1/2 cup of roasted vegetables, 1 slice of whole-grain bread
Afternoon Snack 1 cup of Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup of mixed berries, 1 tablespoon of honey
Dinner 4 oz of grilled salmon, 1 cup of steamed brown rice, 1 cup of steamed vegetables, 1 slice of whole-grain bread
Evening Snack 1 cup of low-fat cottage cheese, 1/2 cup of sliced peaches
